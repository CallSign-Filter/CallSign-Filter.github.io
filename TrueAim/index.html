The basic firearms training course is designed to address the California's firearms laws, firearms safety, ammunition, parts of the firearms, loading and unloading, and marksmanship.

Owning your own firearms is not required for training, however bringing your own is encouraged if you already have one.

CA Concealed Carry Permit (CCW)

This is an approved CCW training course required by the Ventura County Sheriff Department (VCSD).
Students will learn the required training in firearm safety, and the permissible use of a firearm.
The law requires a minimum of 4 hours, and a maximum of 16 hours of training and certification.
Renewal applicants are mandated every 2 years to take a 4 hour training and certification class.

A certificate of satisfactory completion will be issued to students who demonstrate the knowledge and proficiency required by the practical test and the written test.
The written test requires a passing grade of 80%, and the practical requires 100%.

Additional VCSD CCW permit information can be referenced at: http://www.vcsd.org/ccw-permits.php

    REQUIRED EQUIPMENT:
  • Firearm(s) - You must bring all the firearms to be on your permit
  • Ammunition - You may bring your own, or purchase at the range
  • Firearm holster(s) - You must bring the holster for the firearms on your permit

Utah Concealed Carry Permit (CCW)

We provide out of state Utah permit CCW certification for those looking to obtain the permit to carry outside of California (29 other states). Obtaining a Utah CCW permit is much simpler than the California CCW and only requires that you meet minimum requirements and attend the training which is the similar to the material covered in the California CCW course.

More information on Utah's requirements can be found http://bci.utah.gov/

Upon completion of the course you will receive a certificate of completion to be submitted with your application to the Bureau of Criminal Identification.

Defensive Pistol

Note: This course is only available to alumni students and is not for beginners

Are you ready to test your self-defense shooting skills beyond shooting stationary?

Shooting at static targets can improve your marksmanship, but it does very little to prepare you for shooting in a hostile environment where you and the target are likely to be moving. The Defensive Handgun class provides the opportunity for students that are looking to advance their skills in a semi-realistic shooting environment. The Defensive Pistol course is 2.5 hours long and is held on select Saturday and Sundays of the month. Students must have the mandatory equipment and have the ability to perform activities such as moving, kneeling, squatting, and lite running during live fire drills. The goal of the course is to provide you the ability to practice the skills you will rely on to defend your family and yourself in the event of a life or death encounter.

    TRAINING EXERCISES:
  • Presentation from concealed carry
  • Immediate Action Drills (Type I,II,III Malfunction Drills )
  • Speed / Tactical Reloading
  • Multiple target engagement
  • Shooting while moving
  • Shooting at moving targets
  • Shooting from cover
    REQUIRED GEAR:
  • Semi-automatic handgun w/ 3-4 magazines, or revolver with 3-4 speedloaders
  • In or outside the waistband holster and magazine or speedloader pouches
  • Heavy duty belt
  • 250 rounds of ammunition
  • Eye and ear protection
    OPTIONAL/ SUGGESTED GEAR:
  • Concealment garment (Vest, Jacket)
  • Sunscreen
  • Water

About Us

We've been offering firearms training and certification in the San Fernando Valley and Ventura County area for over 12 years.

Our reputation for providing excellent training is backed by our guarantee you will agree, or your training is free!

Whether you are a beginner, or are interested in advance tactical training such as Conceal Carry Weapon training, we can help you. We offer NRA certified Pistol, Rifle and Shotgun classes, and provide California, Utah, Arizona and Florida Conceal Carry Weapon (CCW) certifications.

All training is by appointment. We offer both 1-on-1 training and group classes. Most of our training is performed at a private outdoor target range, but can also be at an indoor range, or even your home depending on your training.

At our private outdoor range, we have the freedom to train in an exclusive, distraction free, and open environment that goes above and beyond what an indoor range can offer.
